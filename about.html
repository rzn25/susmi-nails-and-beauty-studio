Who We Are

Where Beauty Meets Heart

Susmi Nails and Beauty Studio was founded with a simple but powerful vision — to create a space where every person who walks through our door feels welcomed, valued, and leaves looking and feeling absolutely beautiful.

Located in the heart of Sukedhara, Harisiddhi, Lalitpur, our salon has grown into a trusted beauty destination for clients across the Kathmandu Valley. We take pride in offering professional-grade services in a warm, relaxing, and hygienic environment.

From intricate nail art to flawless lash extensions, every service we provide reflects our deep passion for beauty and our dedication to exceeding client expectations.
