About Us

Where Beauty
Meets Passion

Susmi Nails and Beauty Studio is a premium beauty studio in Sukedhara, Harisiddhi, Lalitpur. Founded with the belief that everyone deserves to feel confident and radiant, we've grown into a trusted beauty destination for clients across the Kathmandu Valley.

"Beauty is not just about how you look — it's about how you feel."

Our team of certified artists brings artistry and care to every single service — using only professional-grade products that are gentle on your skin and nails.
